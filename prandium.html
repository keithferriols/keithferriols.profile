CONCEPT
This was a personal design exercise. I wanted to design a fictional restaurant website that feels seamless when it comes to user interaction. Breaking away from the traditional page-to-page navigation, this design explores the idea of single-page navigation. It is always exciting to approach unexplored new ideas.
