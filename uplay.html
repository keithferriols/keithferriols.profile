CONCEPT
I wanted to create an application that would allow users to follow their favorite artists and quickly visualize the hottest trends in the music industry. They would also be able to closely follow the latest news of artists in relation to user’s selection. Running with this idea I designed a page that would allow for seamless interaction between the user and the product.
